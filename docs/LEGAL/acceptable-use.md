# Acceptable Use Policy (DRAFT)

**Effective: [Date]**

This Policy is incorporated by reference into the LastLeg Terms of
Service. By using LastLeg, you agree to follow it.

## Prohibited inventory

You **may not** list:

1. **UK rail Advance tickets** — National Rail Conditions of Travel
   Condition 28 makes Advance tickets strictly non-transferable. Listing
   one is a breach of NRCoT and these Terms (D002).
2. **Flight tickets** — virtually all airline tickets are non-transferable
   under the carrier's terms.
3. **Tickets you do not personally own** — listing on someone else's
   behalf without their written authorisation is prohibited.
4. **Counterfeit, edited, or otherwise tampered tickets** — the PDF must
   be the original issued by the operator, unmodified.

## Prohibited conduct

You **may not**:

1. **Inflate the original-purchase price** to set a higher resale cap.
   We verify against the operator's confirmation email (P2-14/15).
2. **List the same ticket on multiple platforms** simultaneously.
3. **Sell a ticket and use it yourself** — the operator scan-API check
   and our denied-boarding guarantee both catch this; you will be banned
   on the first incident.
4. **Coach a buyer to misrepresent their identity at boarding** — we
   disclose named-ticket risk per D016 and refund denied-boarding via
   the guarantee fund.
5. **Re-sell or share a ticket you bought on LastLeg.**
6. **Manipulate the price-decay system** by withdrawing + re-listing to
   reset the curve.
7. **Create multiple accounts to bypass guarantee-claim limits** (D009).
8. **Use LastLeg for any purpose other than listing or purchasing UK
   coach tickets.**

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Immediate account suspension.
- Forfeiture of pending payouts.
- Permanent ban (including device + payment-method fingerprint).
- Civil or criminal referral where appropriate (e.g. counterfeit
  tickets).

## Reporting

If you see a listing that violates this Policy, email
trust@lastleg.app.
