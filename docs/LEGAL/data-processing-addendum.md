## 1. Subject matter

LastLeg processes personal data ("Customer Data") on behalf of the
Customer in connection with the Services. Categories of data subjects:
the Customer's end users. Categories of personal data: as described in
Annex I.

## 2. Customer responsibilities

The Customer warrants it has a lawful basis (UK GDPR Article 6) for the
personal data it directs LastLeg to process.

## 3. Sub-processors

LastLeg uses the sub-processors listed in our Privacy Policy. We notify
the Customer of changes at least 30 days in advance.

## 4. Security

LastLeg implements appropriate technical and organisational measures,
including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3+) and at rest.
- Role-based access controls with audit logging.
- Regular security reviews.
- Incident response procedures.

## 5. Personal data breach

LastLeg will notify the Customer without undue delay (within 72 hours)
of becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach affecting Customer Data.

## 6. Data subject rights

LastLeg will assist the Customer in responding to data subject requests.

## 7. Term

This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the Services Agreement
plus any retention period required by law.
