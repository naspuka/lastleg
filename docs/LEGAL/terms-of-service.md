## 1. What LastLeg is

We are a marketplace intermediary — we do not sell tickets ourselves, and
we are not the operator of any coach service.

## 5. Fees

## 6. Escrow

Buyer payments are authorised at purchase but **not captured** until
approximately one hour after the scheduled departure. We charge the
buyer's card at that time and transfer the seller's share to their Stripe
Connect account (D012).

## 7. Seller payouts

We use Stripe Connect Express for seller payouts. Sellers must complete
Stripe Identity verification before any payout. Payouts are released
~1 hour after departure unless a guarantee claim is open or operator scan
data indicates the ticket was not used (D015).

## 8. Guarantee fund

For documented failure modes (denied boarding, operator cancellation,
ticket invalid, seller misconduct), our guarantee fund refunds the buyer
in full (ticket price + buyer fee). Each buyer is entitled to up to two
auto-approved claims per lifetime; subsequent claims are manually
reviewed (D009).
